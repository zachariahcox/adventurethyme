2lbs beets, diced into 1/2 in cubes  
2 lbs new potatoes, diced into 1/2 in cubes  
1C diced dill pickles (salt brined preferred)  
1/2 lb carrots diced  
2T Olive oil  
2T minced garlic  
2T minced dill  
2T lemon juice  
1/2 C Greek yogurt / mayonnaise / sour cream  
  
1. Toss beets, potatoes, carrots with olive oil, salt and pepper.  
2. Roast beets potatoes and carrots until cooked, tossing with spatula 3/4 way through (maybe 30 minutes total). Potatoes and carrots will cook faster.  
3. Combine all ingredients adding sour cream to taste
4. Serve with beet greens
